Relisting on eBay

  1. Login to ebay
  2. Click Sell in the top left corner
  3. Click Unsold on the left sidebar
  4. Click the checkbox next to the Actions heading on the table
  5. Scroll down to check if any of the products have variations. If they do uncheck them.
  6. Click Relist
  7. Wait for the products to load. Check the price then click Continue.
  8. It will show you the price one last time, then click Continue again
  9. Click the checkbox nex to the Actions heading on the table and uncheck the items you didn't relist
  10. Click the Action dropdown then Delete
  11. Click Delete again in the dialog that is shown
